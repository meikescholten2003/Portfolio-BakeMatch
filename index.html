Welcome to BakeMatch

A web-based recipe platform that helps users discover what they can cook or bake using only the ingredients they already have at home. BakeMatch makes cooking more accessible, creative, and sustainable by reducing food waste and inspiring people to try new recipes with what they already own.

Visit BakeMatch Overview Project

What This Project Does

BakeMatch is an interactive website that reverses the traditional recipe search process. Instead of finding a recipe and then buying ingredients, users simply enter what they have at home and instantly discover which dishes they can make. The platform combines functionality, simplicity, and creativity to make cooking fun and inspiring.

Ingredient-Based Search

Select the ingredients you have at home, and BakeMatch instantly suggests recipes you can make without shopping.

Recipe Discovery

Browse categories, filter by categories and areas, and explore recipes with step-by-step instructions and images.

Smart Features

Save favorite recipes, get cooking tips for ingredients, and receive help through an integrated AI chatbot.

The Problem It Solves

Many people, especially students and young professionals, often have ingredients at home but don't know what to make with them. Traditional recipe platforms focus on ideal recipes instead of real kitchens, leading to frustration when users realize they're missing half the ingredients.

BakeMatch reverses this logic by starting with what users already have at home. It makes cooking more accessible while helping reduce food waste and encouraging creativity in the kitchen. The platform targets students, young professionals, and beginner cooks who want quick meal inspiration without complex steps or overwhelming interfaces.
