About This Project

Learn about the creator behind BakeMatch and the inspiration for this project.

Welcome to my BakeMatch portfolio! This website was created as a student project to help home cooks and baking enthusiasts make the most of their pantry ingredients and reduce food waste in a fun, practical way.

Meet the Creator

Hi! I'm Meike Scholten, a 22-year-old student from Borne, Netherlands. I'm currently in my third year of the HBO degree in Engineering at Saxion University of Applied Sciences in Enschede. For my minor, I chose Creative, Design, and Technology, which is the context in which BakeMatch was developed.

I've known since high school that I wanted to study Engineering, and I previously completed an MBO Engineering program. My main passion is drawing houses, but I also have a deep love for baking and cooking. That's why I chose this theme for my showcase project—it combines my technical skills with something I'm genuinely passionate about.

Why BakeMatch?

Growing up, I gained a lot of experience with baking and cooking at home, especially from my mother. My sister is studying to become a baker, so I continue to learn new techniques and recipes from her as well.

One problem I kept noticing at home was that we often had leftover ingredients we didn't know how to use. Sometimes these ingredients would expire and end up being thrown away. I wanted to solve this problem in a practical and creative way—and that's how BakeMatch was born.

BakeMatch helps you discover recipes based on the ingredients you already have in your pantry. Whether you have a few random items or a fully stocked kitchen, this tool makes it easy to find delicious recipes, reduce waste, and get inspired in the kitchen.

About This Project

BakeMatch is a student project created as part of the Creative, Design, and Technology minor at Saxion University of Applied Sciences. It showcases web development skills, user-centered design, and practical problem-solving. While it's a learning project, it's built with real functionality to help people make better use of their ingredients and enjoy cooking more.

I hope you enjoy exploring BakeMatch as much as I enjoyed creating it. Happy baking!

— Meike Scholten, 2026
